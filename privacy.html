Introduction

SmartUniTools builds simple utility applications designed to work offline and provide useful everyday tools. This Privacy Policy explains how our applications handle user information.

Information Collection

SmartUniTools applications do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Our apps function completely offline and do not require user accounts, registration, or personal data input.

Permissions

SmartUniTools applications request only the minimum permissions necessary for core functionality. No sensitive device data is accessed or stored.

Third-Party Services

SmartUniTools apps do not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking technologies.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted, user privacy is inherently protected.

Children’s Privacy

SmartUniTools applications are safe for general audiences and do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect improvements or changes in application functionality. Updates will be published on this page.
